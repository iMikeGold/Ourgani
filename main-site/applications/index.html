Portable by Design

Ourgani is not limited to one use case. It is a foundation that can be applied to any initiative — from small teams to large ecosystems, from technology to culture.

Within the Multiplied Intelligence (Xi) Ecosystem

It provides the operational logic that aligns ventures, brands, and services — ensuring coherence while allowing independence.

Independent Organisations

Used by external teams and companies to build their own systems, governance, and intelligence — without being tied to any group.

Community & Not‑For‑Profit

Applied to projects where clarity, resilience, and shared purpose matter more than hierarchy or control.

Specific implementations — such as the 9 Pillar structure used by Multiplied Intelligence (Xi) — are examples of Ourgani in action, not the framework itself.
