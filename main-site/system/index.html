The Ourgani Operating Model

Ourgani is built as a layered framework — each layer represents a distinct type of function, portable across any ecosystem or organisation. These are not specific to one group; they are universal operational principles.

🧠 Intelligence Layer

Purpose: Sense, learn, decide. This is where data becomes understanding, and understanding becomes action. Powered by Multiplied Intelligence methodologies.

Role: Provides context, insight, and adaptive logic to all other layers.

🗂 Knowledge Layer

Purpose: Structure, preserve, connect. How information is organised, stored, and made accessible so it can be reused and evolved over time.

Role: Ensures continuity, clarity, and cumulative learning across the system.

⚙ Operational Layer

Purpose: Execute, deliver, sustain. The engine room — workflows, processes, infrastructure, and service delivery that make things happen reliably.

Role: Translates intent and knowledge into tangible results.

🔗 Integration Layer

Purpose: Connect, align, extend. How different parts of the system (or external systems) communicate, share resources, and remain aligned to common goals.

Role: Removes silos and ensures the whole is greater than the sum of its parts.

🌱 Evolution Layer

Purpose: Grow, adapt, regenerate. The mechanisms for feedback, improvement, innovation, and long‑term health — built in from day one.

Role: Guarantees the system does not just survive, but matures and thrives over time.

Core Methodology: GANI

Every layer operates using the GANI Methodology — the heartbeat of Ourgani:

  1. Learn Your GANI — Understand context, goals, and environment deeply
  2. Apply Our GANI — Embed proven, adaptive operational frameworks
  3. Collective GANI — Create shared intelligence that grows with use

Specific implementations — such as the Gouldbourne ecosystem’s 9‑pillar structure — are applications of this framework, not the framework itself.
