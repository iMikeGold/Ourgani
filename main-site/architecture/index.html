The Five Layers

Ourgani is built as a stack of distinct, reusable layers — applicable to any organisation, project, or ecosystem. These are not tied to any specific brand or group.

🧠 Intelligence Layer

Sense, learn, decide — where data becomes understanding and understanding becomes action.

🗂 Knowledge Layer

Structure, preserve, connect — how information is organised, stored, and made reusable.

⚙ Operational Layer

Execute, deliver, sustain — workflows, infrastructure, and reliable service delivery.

🔗 Integration Layer

Connect, align, extend — how parts communicate, share resources, and stay aligned.

🌱 Evolution Layer

Grow, adapt, regenerate — feedback, improvement, and long‑term health built in.
